Automatically collected information

The following information is collected automatically when you use the system:

  • 15 days Your IP address
  • 15 days Your browser's user agent
  • 15 days The page/service accessed
  • 15 days The date and time of access

These informations are stored for only 15 days. They are stored in raw logs and are not accessed by any other parts of the system.

Twitch cache informations

The following information is stored to prevent excessive calls to the Twitch API:

  • 30 days Twitch user unique ID, username and creation date
  • 1 hour Twitch user profile picture url
  • 30 days Twitch game unique ID
  • 1 hour Twitch team logo thumbnail url
  • 30 days Twitch team unique ID
